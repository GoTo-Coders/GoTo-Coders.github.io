About us

Our organization provides a common platform where all the passionate coders can contribute and uplift themselves as well as the organization. We are an organization of people who focuses on Data Structure, Algorithms, Competitive Programming, projects Development and Web development. It is our goal to provide access to well written, well-explained programs and knowledge that improve people's lives. We want to enable people to adapt and change code according to their own ideas through an Open Access approach. We bring amazing people together to code algorithms and share our work for the benefit of all.

DSA


The Data Structure is Collection and Arrangement of data and its storage in computer memory, so that it can be easily accessed and retrieved when required. An Algorithm is a unambiguous specification of steps to be taken to solve a problem.

CP


Competitive Programming is solving well-defined problems by writing computer programs under specified limits. It is a mind sport usually refers to make efficient algorithms using appropriate data structures. Although directly they do not have any real life significance but they test the skills of programmers

Web Dev


Web Development is the profession involved in developing a website for the Internet. Web development can extend from developing a simple single static page of plain text to complex web-based internet applications, electronic businesses, and social network services.
